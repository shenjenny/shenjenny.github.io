About me

I’m Jenny Shen, an independent UX UI Consultant. I was originally born in Taipei and grew up in Vancouver, where I studied interaction design at Simon Fraser University. In 2013, I moved to Singapore to lead UX/UI design at Gimmie, and in 2015 I moved to Amsterdam to join TravelBird as an Interaction Designer.

Since 2011, I’ve been working with clients across the globe under my personal brand. I’ve built a portfolio of 20+ clients from 10 different countries, designing for users from over 27 countries. Today, I focus on solving difficult UX challenges in the travel, mobile, e-commerce, and SaaS markets.

When I’m not doing commercial work, I love to share my passion for UX and remote work via public speaking. I’m also the Amsterdam chapter founder of Ladies that UX, a UX community of over 1000 members, partnering with companies such as Adobe, Google and Github and international speakers.
